What is this? This heatmap shows the pairwise cosine similarity between the embedding vectors of your search results. Each cell represents how semantically similar two code chunks are to each other in the embedding space. Red/yellow cells indicate high similarity (the chunks are about similar concepts), while blue cells indicate low similarity (different topics). The diagonal is always 100% since each chunk is identical to itself. This helps you spot clusters of related results and understand how diverse your search results are.
